Obviously most of the good contents are not in my native language (french). During the end game of Monster Hunter Wilds most of your time is spent analyzing what is the best build your maximizing your damages, to do so you need to optimize what's called 'decorations' attached to your equipments, there are a lot of it. With this project I'm trying to expose the corresponding translation of en--fr decorations
